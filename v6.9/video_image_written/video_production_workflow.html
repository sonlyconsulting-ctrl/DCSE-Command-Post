📝 Script Architecture & Messaging Framework

🎣 Hook (0-3 seconds)

Purpose: Capture immediate attention and establish relevance

Template Structure:

"What if [specific pain point] could be transformed into [desired outcome] in just [timeframe]?"

Example: "What if business confusion could be transformed into strategic clarity in just 30 days?"

AI Prompt for ChatGPT/Claude:

"Create 5 compelling video hooks for [topic] targeting [audience]. Each hook should be under 10 words, create curiosity, and align with the executive yet warm tone of DCS Enterprise. Include the pain point of [specific challenge] and promise of [specific outcome]."

🎯 Value Proposition (3-15 seconds)

Purpose: Establish credibility and preview the value

Framework:

  • Acknowledge the problem (empathy)
  • Position expertise (authority)
  • Promise specific outcome (value)

📚 Content Delivery (15-85% of video)

Purpose: Deliver core value and build trust

Educational Segments

Chunked into 30-45 second modules

Story Integration

Real examples and case studies

Visual Cues

Screen annotations and graphics

🚀 Call-to-Action (Final 10-15%)

Purpose: Drive specific, measurable action

GYTO™ Integration: "Ready to transform your approach? GET YOUR THINK ON!™ and [specific action]"

  • Visit [specific landing page]
  • Download [specific resource]
  • Schedule [specific consultation]
  • Subscribe for [specific benefit]
