# VIDEO & CONTENT FRAMEWORK DOCTRINE

## Purpose
Govern structure, routing, and output rules for all video and content across DCSE.

## Entity Routing
- SC: business, client, structured messaging
- SS: cinematic, lifestyle, narrative
- TI: instructional, training systems
- PS: controlled, legal-safe only

## Structure Rule
All outputs follow:
1. Opening (hook/context)
2. Body (core message)
3. Close (action/resolution)

## Output Types
- script
- storyboard
- shot list
- production brief

## Rules
- No PS-sensitive leakage into public outputs
- Maintain entity voice discipline
- Outputs must match requested format
- No embedded technical specs

## QA
- clarity
- structure integrity
- entity alignment
- purpose consistency
