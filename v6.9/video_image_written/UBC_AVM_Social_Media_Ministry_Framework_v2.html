🎥 AVM & 📲 Social Media Ministry Framework

Prepared by: Don Seals, Sonly Consulting
Date: December 13, 2025

Guiding Lens ✨

Effective digital ministry depends on people with vision, care, curiosity, and judgment. The congregation is the primary audience.

⛪ We both appreciate the longevity of UBC and the steady tenure of long-serving ministries. This framework introduces durable steps meant to leave an impression for years to come.

Two Connected Systems 🔗

AVM: live production and quality

Social Media: publishing, messaging, engagement

The livestream is the flight. The website and social platforms are the destination.

Enhanced Workflow 🧭

  • Standardize pre-livestream steps
  • Produce livestream
  • Save without auto-publish
  • Edit and quality check
  • Publish intentionally

Phased Growth 🌱

  • Anchor livestream content
  • Archived access
  • Targeted curation
  • Special features
  • Premium initiatives
