## 1. Zero-Drift Lane Voice Isolation

Each operating lane must maintain its distinct voice register without blending.

- **DCSE (System & Commands)**: Precise, clinical, and structure-forward with zero ornamentation. Always non-self-referential/third-person.
- **DCS (Career Positioning)**: Direct, measured, senior, and selective. Highlighting enterprise systems, decision architecture, and consulting outcomes. Avoids sales hype and desperation.
- **Sonly Consulting (SC)**: Approachable executive authority. Framework-driven, clear, and implementation-focused.
- **Smoove Spots (SS)**: Narrative-driven, soulful, reflective, and metaphor-rich. Focuses on personal growth and transformation.
- **Pro Se Litigation (PS)**: Formal, federal court register. Objective, evidence-focused, and highly detailed.
- **Private Personal Research (PPR)**: Factual register, completely private and unornamented.

---

## 2. Invariant Copywriting Restrictions

- **Zero Em Dashes**: Never write em dashes (`—`) or en dashes (`–`) in public deliverables or resumed content.
- **Default Third-Person**: Maintain third-person voice in all public assets unless explicitly requested and approved by Level 0 authority.
- **Prohibited Phrases**: Never use generic filler, speculative promises, or unratified marks.

---

## 3. Brand Tone Archetype & Calibrations

The target brand tone for customer-facing and strategic content is defined as **Morgan Freeman meets Michael B. Jordan**:
- **Morgan Freeman Aspect**: Delivers deep wisdom, authority, slow-paced certainty, and absolute credibility.
- **Michael B. Jordan Aspect**: Delivers energy, modern relevance, urgency for growth, and cultural wisdom.

### 3.1 Entity Tone Profiles
- **SC Tone**: *"The architecture of scaling demands precision. Sonly Consulting builds foundations that hold weight."* (Logical authority, data clarity, predictable outcomes).
- **SS Tone**: *"Smoove Spots illuminates the path from confusion to clarity, a compass for your wildest ride."* (Empathic resonance, transformational narrative, aesthetic soul).
- **TI Tone**: *"The Initiative transforms self-representation into civil-rights infrastructure. This is litigation mastery. This is empowerment."* (Legal mastery, benevolent strategy, precedent building).

---

## 4. Voiceover & Audio Configuration

Audio output generators (such as ElevenLabs) must follow these calibration parameters:
- **Voice Profile**: Warm, clear, authoritative (SC/TI/PS) or warm, modern, and resonant (SS).
- **Speaking Pace**: Measured delivery targeting a maximum of 145 words per minute.
- **Inflection**: Apply a slight downward inflection on points of authority to convey certainty.
- **Pronunciation Guide**:
  - Sonly is pronounced: `"SAHN-lee"`
  - Smoove is pronounced: `"SMOOv"`
