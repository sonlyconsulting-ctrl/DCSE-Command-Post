## 1. Scope of Campaign Governance

Campaign Governance applies exclusively to market-facing positioning, lead generation, and communications.
- **Channels**: Websites, landing pages, email marketing sequences, social media posts, video scripts, and SEO/GEO/AEO assets.
- **Firewall**: Campaign files must remain free of internal architectural documentation, database schemas, and private litigation facts.

---

## 2. SEO, GEO, and AEO Standards

All search optimization assets (Search Engine Optimization, Generative Engine Optimization, and Answer Engine Optimization) must follow strict controlled guidelines:
- **No Em Dashes**: Prohibited in all web metadata, headers, page descriptions, and public paragraphs.
- **Traceability**: All public capability statements must map directly to verified corporate milestones.
- **Tone**: Maintain approachable authority, avoiding generic buzzwords or exaggerated performance claims.
